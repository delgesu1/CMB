Six Extraordinary Settings

Our Venues

From historic sanctuaries to intimate lounges, each space offers a unique encounter with chamber music.

Historic

Tuttle House Concerts

St. Michael's Episcopal Cathedral

Intimate hour-long performances in a historic stone sanctuary, featuring masterworks, contemporary pieces, and hidden gems of the chamber repertoire.

Partnership Tuttle House at St. Michael's & Idaho Concerts in Care
Special Feature "Inside Chamber Music with Bradley Berg" - insights into the masterworks
Admission Suggested donation $30 adults · Students & children free
Experience Complimentary refreshments & meet-the-musicians reception following each concert
Grand

First Presbyterian Church

Downtown Boise

A magnificent sanctuary with soaring wooden arches, exceptional acoustics, and the dramatic backdrop of a grand pipe organ.

Acoustics Exceptional natural reverb from timber-frame construction and vaulted ceilings
Character Traditional concert experience with reverent atmosphere and comfortable seating
Programming Full-length concerts featuring larger ensemble works and solo recitals
Intimate

Lyrical Libations

8 Horses Lounge

Chamber music meets cocktail culture in this sophisticated speakeasy setting, where velvet seating and candlelit ambiance create an unforgettable evening.

Experience Craft cocktails and curated wines paired with intimate musical performances
Atmosphere Plush teal velvet seating, warm candlelight, and art deco elegance
Social A relaxed, conversational setting perfect for newcomers to chamber music
Community

Davis Education Fund Series

St. Stephen's Episcopal Church

A warm, welcoming chapel adorned with beautiful stained glass, dedicated to bringing chamber music education and performance to the community.

Mission Supporting music education through accessible, community-focused concerts
Atmosphere Natural light through stained glass creates a warm, inspirational setting
Community Family-friendly programming with educational components for all ages
Contemporary

Bown Crossing Library

Southeast Boise

Award-winning contemporary architecture meets chamber music in this stunning public library, bringing classical performances to the heart of the community.

Architecture Striking modern design with floor-to-ceiling windows and soaring timber ceilings
Accessibility Free parking, ADA accessible, and centrally located for Southeast Boise families
Programming Family-friendly afternoon concerts in partnership with the Boise Public Library
Exclusive

House Concerts

Private Residences

The most intimate chamber music experience: world-class performances in the warmth of private homes, continuing the centuries-old salon tradition.

Setting Elegant private homes throughout the Treasure Valley, rotating locations
Exclusivity Limited to 30-40 guests for an unparalleled intimate experience
Experience Conversation with musicians, wine reception, and the magic of music in a living room
Join Us Subscribe to our newsletter for exclusive house concert invitations
